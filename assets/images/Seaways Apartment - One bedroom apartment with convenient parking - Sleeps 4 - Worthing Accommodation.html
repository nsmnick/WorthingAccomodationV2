Immerse yourself in luxury at Seaways apartment, where modern comforts meet classic elegance.

This beautifully renovated period property offers stunning sea views, allowing you to wake up to the sound of crashing waves every morning. With its high ceilings and elegant fireplace, the spacious living area exudes style and sophistication.

Accommodating up to 4 guests and providing convenient parking, this apartment is perfect for both families and couples seeking tranquillity by the sea.

The apartment offers a sun terrace.
Step out onto the balcony overlooking the sea, and let yourself be captivated by the beauty that surrounds you. With its contemporary design and modern amenities, this apartment promises an unforgettable holiday experience.
